Privacy Policy

This page describes how KestrelNebula Labs handles information related to this website and general support communications. App-specific privacy practices should also be disclosed in the corresponding Google Play listing or in-app privacy notice where required.

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Scope

This policy applies to the public KestrelNebula Labs website hosted at imadimad1996.github.io and to messages sent to the support contact listed on this site.

2. Information we may receive

We may receive information in the following situations:

  • You send an email or support request, including the content of the message and any attachments you provide.
  • The hosting platform may process basic technical information required to deliver the website, such as request logs.
  • If an app provides its own privacy policy or data disclosure, that app-specific notice governs the app data described there.

3. How information may be used

  • To respond to support questions, bug reports, or privacy inquiries.
  • To investigate technical problems reported by users.
  • To meet legal or security obligations where necessary.

4. Android apps and app-specific disclosures

KestrelNebula Labs may publish multiple Android apps with different features and data needs. When an app collects, uses, or shares data, the relevant disclosures should be described in that app's privacy notice and Google Play Data safety information. If you are reviewing a specific app, use the privacy link associated with that app listing as the authoritative app-specific reference.

5. Retention and security

  • Support emails may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to handle the request and maintain records.
  • Information is not intentionally kept longer than needed for support, security, or legal reasons.
  • Reasonable steps may be taken to protect information shared through support communications, but no internet transmission is completely risk-free.

6. Requests, deletion, and contact

For privacy questions, support issues, or account-related deletion requests where an app supports accounts, contact KestrelNebula Labs using the support email below. Include the app name and enough information to identify the request.
